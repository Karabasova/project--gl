Memory game

Game rules:

  1. Mix up small cards.
  2. They will be laid in rows and faced down.
  3. Turn over any two small cards.
  4. If they match then keep them.
  5. If there is not match then turn small cards back over.
  6. Remember text or picture and positions of the cards.
  7. Second player will turn over small cards.
  8. Memory game is over when all small cards have been matched.
  9. The player with the most matches of the cards becomes winner.
